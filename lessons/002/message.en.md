🇪🇸 *Lesson 2 — Spanish Transfer*

*Main idea:*
You can convert thousands of English words ending in \-AL into Spanish by keeping the spelling and shifting stress to the final syllable\.

*New constructions:*
\- *es* — is/he is/she is/it is/you are \(formal\)
\- *no es* — isn't/is not

*Remember:*
Pronounce every vowel exactly as written: A\=ah, E\=eh, I\=ee, O\=oh, U\=oo\. Always stress the final syllable in \-AL words\. The S in "es" is always S, never a Z sound\.

*Example:*
_It's not normal_ — No es normal

💡 Don't guess with grammar structure, but DO guess with vocabulary\. When someone corrects you, that's how you learn fastest\. If you know "verbal," you can guess "verb" is probably "verbo\."
