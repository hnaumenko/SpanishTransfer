🇪🇸 *Lesson 4: Steal 1000\+ verbs in 30 seconds*

You just unlocked over a thousand Spanish verbs\. No memorization needed\.

Here's the pattern: English words ending in *\-tion* become Spanish words ending in *\-ción*\. Then remove the *\-ción*, add *\-r*, and boom — you've got the verb\.

*preparation* → *preparación* → *preparar* \(to prepare\)
*confirmation* → *confirmación* → *confirmar* \(to confirm\)
*generation* → *generación* → *generar* \(to generate\)

Now combine this with *quiero* \(I want\):

— What are your plans tonight?
— *Quiero prepararme\.* I want to get ready\. *No quiero complicar\.* I don't want to complicate things\.

Here are 8 more you already know:

_cancelación / cancelar_ — cancellation / to cancel
_exploración / explorar_ — exploration / to explore
_cooperación / cooperar_ — cooperation / to cooperate
_conversación / conversar_ — conversation / to converse
_confrontación / confrontar_ — confrontation / to confront
_organización / organizar_ — organization / to organize
_participación / participar_ — participation / to participate
_celebración / celebrar_ — celebration / to celebrate

💡 Say *quiero cancelar* \(I want to cancel\), not *quiero a cancelar*\. No extra words needed\.
