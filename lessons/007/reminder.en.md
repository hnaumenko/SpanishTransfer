🌙 *Lesson 7 — reminder*

Movement verbs \(_voy_, _pasar_\) take *a* before the next verb\. Others \(_quiero_, _intento_\) don't\.

_Voy a continuar_ — I'm going to continue\.
_Voy a pasar a verte_ — I'm going to come see you\.
_Quiero mostrarte_ — I want to show you\.
_Intento ver_ — I try to see\.

Movement \= extra *a*\. That's your rule\.
