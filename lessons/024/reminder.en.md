🌙 *Lesson 24 — reminder*

_Dar_ — to give\. Forms: _doy_, _das_, _da_, _dan_\. Pronouns stack: _me lo das_ — are you giving it to me?

_Te lo doy mañana_ — I'll give it to you tomorrow\.
_Me da algo_ — he gives me something\.
_Le damos el libro_ — we give him the book\.

Trick: no need for future tense, just add a time word\.
