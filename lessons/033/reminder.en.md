🌙 Lesson 33 — reminder

*Estar* \+ adjective \= temporary state\. *Ser* \+ adjective \= permanent characteristic\. Same word, opposite life\.

_Estoy listo_ — I'm ready\.
_Soy listo_ — I'm smart\.
_Está feliz_ — Happy now\.
_Es feliz_ — Happy person\.

Safety net: *Soy una persona feliz* works perfectly\.
