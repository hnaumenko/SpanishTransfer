🌙 *Lesson 58 — reminder*

Simple past for \-ar verbs: drop \-ar and add \-é \(I\) or \-ó \(he/she\)\. The accent is essential — it marks past tense\.

_Hablé_ — I spoke\.
_Habló_ — he spoke\.
_Compré_ — I bought\.
_Compró_ — she bought\.
_Olvidé_ — I forgot\.
_Olvidó_ — he forgot\.

The accent matters\. Say them aloud to hear the stress difference\.
