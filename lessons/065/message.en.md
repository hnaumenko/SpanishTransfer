🇪🇸 *Lesson 65: The Secret Life of "Más"*

You already know how to say "more"\. But did you know *más* can also mean *else*?

Here's the pattern:
*nada* \(nothing\) \+ *más* \= nothing else
*nadie* \(nobody\) \+ *más* \= nobody else

Picture this:
— Did you eat anything else?
— *No comí nada más\.* I didn't eat anything else\.

Watch how this unlocks tons of everyday phrases:

_Nada más_ — nothing else
_Nadie más_ — nobody else
_Una vez más_ — one more time
_Otra vez_ — again
_No vi nada más_ — I didn't see anything else
_Quiero verlos una vez más_ — I want to see them one more time
_No llegó nadie más_ — Nobody else arrived
_¿No comiste nada más?_ — Didn't you eat anything else?

💡 Remember: Spanish loves double negatives\. *No comí nada más* literally says "I didn't eat nothing else" — and that's perfectly correct\.

👉 Say this out loud right now: *"No encontré nada más en tu casa\."* \(I didn't find anything else in your house\.\)
