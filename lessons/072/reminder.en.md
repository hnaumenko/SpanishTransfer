🌙 Lesson 72 — reminder

Commands\: negative = subjunctive\. Positive informal = drop \-s from *tú* form\. *No lo des* \(don't give it\), *dalo* \(give it\)\. Objects stick to positive commands\.

_No hables_ — don't speak\.
_Come_ — eat\.
_Cómelo_ — eat it\.
_Olvídalo_ — forget about it\.

Say: "Forget about it\."
