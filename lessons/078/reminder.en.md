🌙 Lesson 78 — reminder

_Seguir_, _empezar_, and _terminar_ are picky verbs\. They take either gerunds or infinitives\.

_Sigo estudiando_ — I keep studying\.
_Empiezo a trabajar_ — I start to work\.
_Termino comiendo_ — I end up eating\.

Listen for these verbs in Spanish today — they're everywhere\.
