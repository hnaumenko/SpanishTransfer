🌙 Lesson 80 — reminder

_Se_ \+ _me/te/le_ turns accidents into things that happen *to* you, not *by* you\.

_Se me quemó la cena_ — The dinner burnt on me\.
_Se le rompió_ — His phone broke on him\.
_Se te olvidó_ — You forgot\.

Think of one thing that happened *to* you this week that you didn't cause\.
