🌙 Lesson 84 — reminder

GO\-verbs add _\-go_ only in the "I" form\: _tengo, vengo, salgo, pongo_\.

_Tengo_ — I have\.
_Vengo_ — I come\.
_Salgo_ — I leave\.
_Pongo_ — I put\.

In the subjunctive, that _\-g_ spreads to everyone\: _venga, salga, ponga, haga, diga_\.

Say out loud: "No quiero que me distraigas\."
