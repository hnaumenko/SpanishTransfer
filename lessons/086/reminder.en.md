🌙 *Lesson 86 — reminder*

GO\-verbs contract in the future\. The E becomes D\: _tener_ → _tendré_\.

_Vendré mañana_ — I'll come tomorrow\.
_Saldría pero no tendré tiempo_ — I would leave but I won't have time\.
_Tendrás que estudiar_ — You'll have to study\.

Say three: "Tendré\.\.\.", "Vendrás\.\.\.", "Saldrá\.\.\."
